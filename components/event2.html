KIMS HOSPITAL,ONGOLE

NOTICE

Blood Donation Camp

Date: March 20, 2025

We are pleased to announce that KIMS Hospital, Ongole, is organizing a Blood Donation Camp on March 20, 2025. The camp will be held from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM at the hospital premises.

Your participation can make a difference! Blood cannot be manufactured; it can only be given by generous donors like you. A single donation can save up to three lives. Your support is crucial in making this event a success.
