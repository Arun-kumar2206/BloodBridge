Blood Donation Eligibility Notice

  • Eligibility: Age 18–65, weight ≥50 kg, hemoglobin ≥12.5 g/dL, and in good health.
  • Ineligibility: Chronic diseases, recent surgery/tattoo (6 months), alcohol (24 hours), pregnancy.
  • Before Donation: Stay hydrated, eat light, avoid alcohol and smoking (24 hours).
  • After Donation: Rest 15 minutes, avoid heavy exercise for 24 hours.
